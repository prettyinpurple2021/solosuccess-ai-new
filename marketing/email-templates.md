## Email Design Guidelines

### Visual Style
- **Header**: SoloSuccess AI logo with glassmorphic background
- **Colors**: Deep blues, purples, teals (brand gradient)
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif
- **Buttons**: Glassmorphic style with gradient hover states
- **Footer**: Minimal with social links and legal

### Technical Specs
- **Width**: 600px max
- **Mobile Responsive**: Yes
- **Dark Mode**: Support both light and dark
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
- **Testing**: Litmus for email client compatibility

### Personalization Tokens
- `[First Name]`
- `[Plan]` (Free/Accelerator/Premium)
- `[Usage Stats]` (messages sent, sessions completed)
- `[Most Used Agent]`
- `[Days Since Signup]`
- `[Next Billing Date]`

### Unsubscribe Options
All marketing emails include:
- Unsubscribe from this type of email
- Unsubscribe from all marketing emails
- Update email preferences
- Manage subscription

### Sending Schedule
- **Welcome Series**: Triggered by signup
- **Upgrade Emails**: Triggered by usage limits
- **Feature Announcements**: Monthly
- **Educational Content**: Weekly
- **Transactional**: Immediate
- **Re-engagement**: After 14 days inactive
