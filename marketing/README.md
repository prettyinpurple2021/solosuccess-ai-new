Complete copy for the main landing page including:
- Hero section with headline and CTAs

## Brand Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- **Professional yet approachable**: We're experts, but we speak like humans
- **Empowering**: Focus on what users can achieve
- **Confident**: We believe in our product
- **Supportive**: We understand the solo founder struggle
- **Clear and concise**: No fluff, just value

### Visual Identity
- **Colors**: Deep blues (#1E3A8A), purples (#7C3AED), teals (#0D9488)
- **Design Style**: 3D glassmorphism with dynamic gradients
- **Typography**: Inter (body), Poppins (headings)
- **Effects**: Frosted glass, smooth animations, elegant hover states

### Key Messaging

**Primary Value Proposition**:
"Your Virtual Executive Team. Powered by AI."

**Core Benefits**:
1. Expert guidance across all business functions
2. 24/7 availability without hiring overhead
3. Intelligent automation that saves time
4. Strategic insights that drive growth

**Target Audience**:
- Solo founders and solopreneurs
- Early-stage startup founders (pre-seed to seed)
- Entrepreneurs building without co-founders
- Small business owners seeking to scale

## Content Production Workflow

### Weekly Content Creation
1. **Monday**: Plan week's content based on calendar
2. **Tuesday**: Write copy for all posts
3. **Wednesday**: Create visual assets
4. **Thursday**: Schedule posts for following week
5. **Friday**: Engage with community and respond to comments

### Monthly Content Review
- Analyze performance metrics
- Identify top-performing content
- Adjust strategy based on data
- Plan next month's content themes
- Update templates as needed

## Metrics & KPIs

### Website Metrics
- **Traffic**: Unique visitors, page views
- **Conversion**: Trial signups, paid conversions
- **Engagement**: Time on site, bounce rate
- **SEO**: Organic search rankings, keyword performance

### Email Metrics
- **Deliverability**: Open rate (target: 25%+)
- **Engagement**: Click-through rate (target: 5%+)
- **Conversion**: Trial signups, upgrades
- **List Growth**: New subscribers per week

### Social Media Metrics
- **Reach**: Impressions, followers
- **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares (target: 3%+ engagement rate)
- **Traffic**: Click-throughs to website
- **Conversion**: Trial signups from social

### Paid Advertising Metrics
- **Cost per Click**: Target: <$2
- **Cost per Acquisition**: Target: <$50
- **Return on Ad Spend**: Target: 3:1
- **Conversion Rate**: Target: 5%+
