# Social Media Assets & Content Strategy

## Platform Strategy Overview

### LinkedIn (Primary Platform)
**Target**: Solo founders, entrepreneurs, startup founders
**Tone**: Professional, insightful, strategic
**Frequency**: 5x per week
**Content Mix**: 40% educational, 30% product features, 20% social proof, 10% company updates

### Twitter/X (Secondary Platform)
**Target**: Tech-savvy founders, startup community
**Tone**: Conversational, witty, fast-paced
**Frequency**: 3x per day
**Content Mix**: 50% quick tips, 30% engagement, 20% product updates

### Instagram (Tertiary Platform)
**Target**: Visual-oriented founders, lifestyle entrepreneurs
**Tone**: Inspiring, aesthetic, aspirational
**Frequency**: 3x per week
**Content Mix**: 60% visual quotes, 30% behind-the-scenes, 10% product demos
