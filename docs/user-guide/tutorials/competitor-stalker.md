Learn how to track competitors and receive automated intelligence briefings.

## What is Competitor Stalker?

Competitor Stalker is your automated competitive intelligence system. It monitors your competitors across multiple channels and delivers daily AI-powered briefings with actionable insights.

**Think of it as:** Having a dedicated market intelligence analyst working 24/7.

**Strategic Benefits:**
- Identify market opportunities before competitors
- Anticipate competitive moves
- Benchmark your performance
- Inform product and pricing decisions
- Stay ahead of industry trends

**Time Savings:**
- Automated monitoring vs. manual research
- Daily briefings vs. ad-hoc checking
- AI-powered analysis vs. raw data

## Real-World Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Pricing Strategy

**Scenario:**
You notice 3 competitors lowered their prices in the same week.

**Action:**
1. Review pricing changes in detail
2. Analyze your value proposition
3. Ask Blaze: "Competitors dropped prices 20%. Should I match or differentiate?"
4. Create Mission Control session: "Develop pricing strategy response"
5. Implement recommended approach

**Outcome:**
Instead of matching price cuts, you differentiate on value and add premium features.

### Use Case 2: Feature Parity

**Scenario:**
Main competitor launches AI-powered analytics feature.

**Action:**
1. Review feature announcement details
2. Assess customer demand for similar feature
3. Ask Vex: "Technical requirements for similar analytics feature"
4. Evaluate build vs. buy vs. ignore
5. Update product roadmap accordingly

**Outcome:**
Informed decision on product strategy with competitive context.

### Use Case 3: Market Opportunity

**Scenario:**
Notice competitor stopped serving a specific market segment.

**Action:**
1. Investigate why they exited
2. Assess opportunity size
3. Ask Echo: "Should we target [segment] that competitor abandoned?"
4. Create Mission Control: "Market entry strategy for [segment]"
5. Execute if opportunity is validated

**Outcome:**
Capture market share in underserved segment.

## Troubleshooting

### Competitor Not Being Tracked

**Possible Causes:**
- Website blocks automated access
- Invalid URL provided
- Social media profiles are private
- Tracking sources not configured

**Solutions:**
- Verify URL is correct and accessible
- Check tracking source settings
- Try alternative monitoring sources
- Contact support for assistance

### Missing Important Updates

**Possible Causes:**
- Alert settings too restrictive
- Update didn't match tracking rules
- Monitoring frequency too low

**Solutions:**
- Review and adjust alert settings
- Increase monitoring frequency
- Add more tracking sources
- Create custom tracking rules

### Too Many Alerts

**Possible Causes:**
- Tracking too many competitors
- Alert sensitivity too high
- All activities set to high priority

**Solutions:**
- Focus on top 5 competitors
- Adjust alert priority levels
- Use daily digest instead of real-time
- Refine tracking rules

### Briefings Not Arriving

**Solutions:**
- Check email spam folder
- Verify email address in settings
- Check notification preferences
- Ensure subscription is active
- Contact support if issue persists
