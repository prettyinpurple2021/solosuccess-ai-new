# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is SoloSuccess AI?

SoloSuccess AI is an AI-powered platform that provides solo founders and entrepreneurs with a virtual executive team of specialized AI agents. Each agent helps with different aspects of running a business, from strategy to marketing to legal documents.

### Who is SoloSuccess AI for?

SoloSuccess AI is designed for:
- Solo founders building startups
- Freelancers and consultants
- Small business owners
- Side project entrepreneurs
- Anyone running a business without a full team

### How is this different from ChatGPT?

While ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI, SoloSuccess AI offers:
- **Specialized Agents:** Each agent is trained for specific business functions
- **Context Retention:** Agents remember your business details across conversations
- **Integrated Tools:** Competitor tracking, analytics, document generation
- **Mission Control:** Multiple agents collaborating on complex projects
- **Business Focus:** Optimized specifically for entrepreneurial needs

### Do I need technical skills to use SoloSuccess AI?

No technical skills required! The platform is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly. If you can use email or social media, you can use SoloSuccess AI.

## Troubleshooting

### I'm not receiving emails

Check your spam folder. Add noreply@solosuccess.ai to your contacts. If issues persist, contact support.

### Agent responses are slow

Complex queries may take 10-15 seconds. If responses consistently take longer, check your internet connection or contact support.

### I can't log in

Try these steps:
1. Verify you're using the correct email
2. Reset your password
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if issues persist

### My payment failed

Common reasons:
- Insufficient funds
- Expired card
- Incorrect billing address
- Bank declined transaction

Update your payment method in Settings → Subscription → Payment Method.

### I found a bug

Report bugs at bugs.solosuccess.ai or email support@solosuccess.ai. Include:
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Screenshots if possible
- Browser and device information
