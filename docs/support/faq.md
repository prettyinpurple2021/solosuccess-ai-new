# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is SoloSuccess AI?

SoloSuccess AI is an AI-powered platform designed specifically for solopreneurs and small business owners. It provides a team of specialized AI agents to help with strategy, marketing, sales, legal documents, technical architecture, and more.

### Who is SoloSuccess AI for?

SoloSuccess AI is perfect for:
- Solopreneurs building their first business
- Small business owners wearing multiple hats
- Freelancers looking to scale
- Entrepreneurs who need strategic guidance without hiring a full team

### How is SoloSuccess AI different from ChatGPT?

While ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI, SoloSuccess AI offers:
- Specialized AI agents trained for specific business functions
- Mission Control for coordinating multiple agents on complex projects
- Built-in business tools (competitor tracking, content generation, document creation)
- Context retention across conversations
- Business-specific templates and frameworks

### Can I change my subscription tier?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time:
1. Go to Settings > Subscription
2. Select your desired tier
3. Confirm the change

**Upgrades**: Take effect immediately, prorated billing
**Downgrades**: Take effect at the end of your current billing period

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to Settings > Subscription
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period

### Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers. If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days, contact support@solosuccess.ai for a full refund.

### How do I choose which agent to talk to?

Each agent has specific expertise. Choose based on your need:
- Strategy questions → Roxy
- Marketing and growth → Echo
- Sales and funnels → Blaze
- Legal documents → Lumi
- Technical questions → Vex
- Data analysis → Lexi

### Can agents remember previous conversations?

Yes! Each agent maintains conversation history and context. They can reference previous discussions and build on past work.

### What is Mission Control?

Mission Control is a feature where you define a complex objective, and multiple AI agents collaborate to create a comprehensive plan. For example, "Launch my SaaS product in 90 days" would involve Roxy (strategy), Echo (marketing), Blaze (sales), Vex (technical), and Nova (design) working together.

## Features

### What is Competitor Stalker?

Competitor Stalker monitors your competitors' online activities:
- Website changes
- Social media posts
- Product launches
- Pricing changes
- Content updates

You receive daily intelligence briefings and real-time alerts for significant changes.

### How does content generation work?

1. Select content type (social post, blog, email, etc.)
2. Specify platform and audience
3. Provide brief or key points
4. AI generates 3+ variations
5. Edit and refine as needed
6. Save to content library or export

### What is Shadow Self?

Shadow Self is a cognitive bias assessment tool that:
- Identifies your decision-making blind spots
- Reveals unconscious biases
- Provides personalized coaching
- Offers ongoing reflection exercises
- Tracks your growth over time

### What is Chaos/Failure Mode?

Chaos Mode runs "pre-mortem" simulations on your business initiatives:
- Identifies potential failure points
- Assesses likelihood and impact
- Generates mitigation strategies
- Creates contingency plans

It helps you prepare for problems before they happen.

### What documents can I generate?

Lumi can help generate:
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policies
- Service Agreements
- NDAs (Non-Disclosure Agreements)
- Independent Contractor Agreements
- Basic contracts

**Important**: These are templates, not legal advice. Always consult a licensed attorney for legal matters.

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

1. Go to login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email
4. Check email for reset link
5. Create new password

### I'm not receiving emails from SoloSuccess AI

Check:
1. Spam/junk folder
2. Email filters
3. Verify email address in Settings
4. Add support@solosuccess.ai to contacts

Still not working? Contact support@solosuccess.ai

### The AI agent isn't responding

Try:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Check status.solosuccess.ai for outages

If problem persists, contact support.

### I'm getting an error message

1. Note the exact error message
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try incognito/private mode
5. Contact support with error details

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Scroll to "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion
4. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

**Note**: This action cannot be undone.
